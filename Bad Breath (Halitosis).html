DISEASE DIRECTORY Oral and Dental Issues

BAD BREATH (HALITOSIS)

Symptoms

Bad breath, also known as halitosis, is an unpleasant odor from the mouth. It can be caused by poor dental hygiene, food particles, dry mouth, or underlying health issues.

Remedies

  • Brush your teeth, gums, and tongue at least twice a day to remove bacteria.
  • Floss daily to remove food particles between teeth.
  • Rinse with an antiseptic mouthwash to kill bacteria that cause bad breath.
  • Stay hydrated to avoid dry mouth, which can contribute to bad breath.
  • Chew sugar-free gum or suck on sugar-free mints to stimulate saliva production.
