BALANCED DIET

A balanced diet is essential for maintaining overall health and well-being. It involves consuming a variety of foods in the right proportions to provide the necessary nutrients your body needs. This includes a mix of carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals. By incorporating fruits, vegetables, lean meats, whole grains, and dairy into your meals, you ensure that your body receives all the essential nutrients. A balanced diet helps in energy regulation, promotes healthy growth, supports immune function, and can prevent chronic diseases.
