CARDIOVASCULAR EXERCISE

Cardiovascular exercise, often referred to as cardio, involves activities that increase the heart rate and improve the efficiency of the cardiovascular system. Common forms of cardio include running, cycling, swimming, and brisk walking. These exercises help to strengthen the heart, improve lung capacity, and enhance overall endurance. Engaging in regular cardiovascular exercise can also aid in weight management, boost mood, and reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease and diabetes. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity or 75 minutes of high-intensity cardio per week for optimal health benefits.
