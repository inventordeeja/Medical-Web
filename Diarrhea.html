DISEASE DIRECTORY Digestive Issues

DIARRHEA

Symptoms

Diarrhea is a digestive condition characterized by frequent, loose, watery stools. It can be caused by a variety of factors, including infections, food poisoning, medication side effects, and underlying medical conditions. Symptoms of diarrhea may include abdominal cramps, fever, nausea, and dehydration.

Remedies

  • Stay hydrated by drinking water, clear broths, or oral rehydration solutions.
  • Eat bland, easy-to-digest foods like bananas, rice, applesauce, and toast (BRAT diet).
  • Avoid dairy, greasy, and high-fiber foods until diarrhea subsides.
  • Take over-the-counter anti-diarrheal medications like loperamide (Imodium).
  • Rest and avoid stressful activities.
