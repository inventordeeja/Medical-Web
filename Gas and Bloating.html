DISEASE DIRECTORY Digestive Issues

GAS AND BLOATING

Symptoms

Gas and bloating are common digestive problems characterized by excessive gas production and abdominal swelling. Symptoms of gas and bloating may include abdominal pain, discomfort, and a feeling of fullness.

Remedies

  • Avoid carbonated beverages and chewing gum.
  • Eat slowly to reduce swallowed air.
  • Incorporate ginger, peppermint tea, or fennel seeds into your routine for relief.
  • Take over-the-counter medications like simethicone to reduce gas.
  • Engage in light physical activity like walking after meals to aid digestion.
