GUM DISEASE (GINGIVITIS)

Symptoms

Gum disease, also known as gingivitis, is an inflammation of the gums caused by bacteria buildup. Symptoms of gum disease include red, swollen, and bleeding gums, as well as bad breath.

Remedies

  • Brush your teeth twice a day and floss daily to remove plaque from teeth and gums.
  • Use an antiseptic mouthwash to reduce bacteria and plaque buildup.
  • Rinse with warm salt water to reduce gum inflammation and promote healing.
  • Avoid smoking, which can worsen gum disease.
  • Schedule regular dental cleanings to remove tartar buildup that contributes to gingivitis.
