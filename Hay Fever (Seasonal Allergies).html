HAY FEVER (SEASONAL ALLERGIES)

Symptoms

Hay fever, also known as allergic rhinitis, is a common condition caused by an allergic reaction to pollen from plants, trees, or grasses. Symptoms typically occur during specific seasons, such as spring, summer, or fall, and can significantly impact a personâ€™s quality of life. Common symptoms of hay fever include sneezing, runny nose, itchy eyes and nose, congestion, and post-nasal drip.

Remedies

  • Stay indoors during peak pollen times (morning and windy days).
  • Use a saline nasal spray to clear allergens from your nasal passages.
  • Take over-the-counter antihistamines to relieve symptoms.
  • Wear sunglasses and a mask outdoors to reduce pollen exposure.
  • Use a dehumidifier and air purifier at home to keep air clean.
