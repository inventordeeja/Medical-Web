HEALTHY EATING FOR WEIGHT MANAGEMENT

Adopting healthy eating habits is crucial for effective weight management. This involves focusing on nutrient-dense foods that are low in empty calories. Incorporating plenty of vegetables, fruits, lean proteins, and whole grains can help control calorie intake while still providing essential nutrients. Additionally, practicing portion control and reducing intake of sugary and high-fat foods are key strategies. Regular meals and snacks can help maintain metabolic rate and prevent overeating, making it easier to achieve and sustain a healthy weight.
