HEARTBURN

Symptoms

Heartburn is a common digestive problem characterized by a burning sensation in the chest. It occurs when stomach acid flows back up into the esophagus, a tube that connects the mouth to the stomach. This condition is often referred to as acid reflux. Symptoms of heartburn may include a bitter taste in the mouth, difficulty swallowing, and chest pain.

Remedies

  • Use over-the-counter antacids to neutralize stomach acid.
  • Drink a glass of water mixed with a teaspoon of baking soda (sodium bicarbonate) for quick relief.
