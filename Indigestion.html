DISEASE DIRECTORY Digestive Issues

INDIGESTION

Symptoms

Indigestion is a common digestive complaint characterized by discomfort or pain in the upper abdomen. It often occurs after eating and may be accompanied by bloating, belching, and heartburn.

Remedies

  • Eat smaller, slower meals to give your stomach time to digest.
  • Avoid foods that trigger your symptoms, such as fatty or spicy foods.
  • Drink ginger tea or chew on fresh ginger to soothe the stomach.
  • Use over-the-counter antacids to relieve discomfort.
  • Stay upright for at least an hour after eating to help digestion.
