DISEASE DIRECTORY Minor Aches and Pains

JOINT PAIN

Symptoms

Joint pain can be caused by arthritis, injury, or overuse, leading to pain, stiffness, swelling, and difficulty moving the joint.

Remedies

  • Rest the joint and avoid overuse.
  • Apply ice or a cold compress to reduce inflammation.
  • Use heat therapy for stiff joints.
  • Take over-the-counter pain relievers if needed.
  • Engage in low-impact exercises to maintain joint flexibility.
