LARYNGITIS

Symptoms

Laryngitis is an inflammation of the larynx, or voice box, often caused by a viral infection. Symptoms include a hoarse voice or loss of voice, and it can be particularly bothersome for those who rely on their voices for work or communication.

Remedies

  • Rest your voice as much as possible.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking water or warm herbal teas.
  • Avoid whispering, as it strains the vocal cords.
  • Inhale steam or use a humidifier to keep the throat moist.
  • Suck on throat lozenges or gargle with warm salt water.
