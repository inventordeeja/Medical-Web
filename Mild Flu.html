DISEASE DIRECTORY Cold and Flu

MILD FLU

Symptoms

The flu, or influenza, is a common respiratory illness caused by a virus. Symptoms often include fever, chills, cough, sore throat, runny or stuffy nose, muscle aches, fatigue, and headache.

Remedies

  • Rest and drink plenty of fluids.
  • Use a humidifier to ease breathing.
  • Drink warm teas with honey to soothe your throat.
  • Take over-the-counter flu medications for symptom relief.
  • Use steam inhalation to reduce nasal congestion.
