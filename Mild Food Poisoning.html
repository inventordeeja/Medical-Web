MILD FOOD POISONING

Symptoms

Mild food poisoning is a common illness caused by consuming contaminated food or water. It is often characterized by symptoms such as nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, abdominal cramps, and fever.

Remedies

  • Stay hydrated by drinking water, clear broth, or oral rehydration solutions.
  • Eat bland foods like toast, crackers, and bananas as you recover.
  • Avoid dairy, caffeine, and alcohol until symptoms subside.
  • Rest and give your body time to recover.
  • Seek medical attention if symptoms are severe or last more than a day or two.
