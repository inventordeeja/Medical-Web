DISEASE DIRECTORY Minor Aches and Pains

MUSCLE STRAIN

Symptoms

A muscle strain happens when a muscle is overstretched or torn, usually caused by sudden movements, overuse, or trauma. Symptoms include pain, swelling, bruising, and reduced movement.

Remedies

  • Rest the affected muscle and avoid painful activities.
  • Apply ice to reduce swelling, then use heat therapy after a few days.
  • Gently stretch the muscle to promote healing.
  • Use over-the-counter pain relievers if needed.
