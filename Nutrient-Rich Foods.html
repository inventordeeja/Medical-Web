NUTRIENT-RICH FOODS

Nutrient-rich foods provide a high concentration of essential nutrients with relatively low calories. These foods include a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats. For instance, leafy greens like spinach and kale are packed with vitamins and minerals, while nuts and seeds provide healthy fats and protein. Incorporating nutrient-rich foods into your diet helps ensure you receive the necessary nutrients to support bodily functions, boost immunity, and maintain energy levels. A diet rich in these foods can contribute to overall health and help prevent chronic diseases.
