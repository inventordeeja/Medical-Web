ORAL HYGIENE

Oral hygiene is essential for maintaining healthy teeth and gums and preventing dental problems. It involves regular practices such as brushing teeth at least twice a day with fluoride toothpaste, flossing daily to remove plaque between teeth, and using mouthwash to help kill bacteria and freshen breath. Regular dental check-ups and professional cleanings are also important for detecting and addressing issues early. Good oral hygiene helps prevent cavities, gum disease, and bad breath, and contributes to overall health and well-being.
