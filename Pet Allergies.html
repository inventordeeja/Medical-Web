PET ALLERGIES

Symptoms

Pet allergies are caused by exposure to animal dander, which is a type of protein found in the skin cells of animals. Symptoms of pet allergies can be similar to those of other allergies, such as sneezing, runny nose, itchy eyes and nose, congestion, and wheezing.

Remedies

  • Keep pets out of the bedroom and off furniture.
  • Bathe pets regularly to reduce dander.
  • Use HEPA air filters in your home to trap allergens.
  • Wash hands after petting animals and avoid touching your face.
  • Take over-the-counter antihistamines to manage symptoms.
