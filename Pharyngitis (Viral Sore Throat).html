PHARYNGITIS (VIRAL SORE THROAT)

Symptoms

Pharyngitis, or a viral sore throat, is a common complaint characterized by pain or discomfort in the back of the throat. Symptoms may also include difficulty swallowing, swollen lymph nodes in the neck, and a fever.

Remedies

  • Gargle with warm salt water to reduce throat irritation.
  • Drink warm teas with honey to soothe the throat.
  • Use over-the-counter pain relievers like ibuprofen for pain and fever.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Avoid smoking and exposure to irritants.
