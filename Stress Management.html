STRESS MANAGEMENT

Managing stress is crucial for maintaining both physical and mental health. Effective stress management involves a variety of strategies including regular exercise, a healthy diet, sufficient sleep, and mindfulness practices. These strategies help reduce the impact of stress on your body, improve your mood, and increase your resilience to stressors. Finding hobbies, practicing deep breathing, or even talking to a professional can also play a vital role in keeping stress at bay.
