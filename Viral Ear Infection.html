VIRAL EAR INFECTION

Symptoms

Viral ear infection occurs when the middle ear, located behind the eardrum, becomes inflamed due to a viral infection. Symptoms include ear pain, fever, and hearing loss.

Remedies

  • Apply a warm compress to the affected ear to relieve pain.
  • Take over-the-counter pain relievers like ibuprofen.
  • Stay hydrated and get plenty of rest.
  • Use a saline nasal spray to help reduce ear pressure.
  • Avoid inserting anything into the ear.
