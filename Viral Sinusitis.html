VIRAL SINUSITIS

Symptoms

Viral sinusitis is a common condition that occurs when the sinuses, located behind the forehead, cheekbones, and nose, become inflamed due to a viral infection. Symptoms often include facial pressure, congestion, and a runny or stuffy nose.

Remedies

  • Use a saline nasal spray or rinse.
  • Apply a warm compress to the face to relieve sinus pressure.
  • Drink plenty of fluids to thin mucus.
  • Inhale steam to clear sinus congestion.
  • Use a dehumidifier and air purifier at home to keep air clean.
